The Palace

One day as the King wandered around his palace, he heard loud snoring sounds coming from a rose bush far away. Looking closer, he found a sleeping Satyr, which managed to get lost. The King knew that this Satyr was one of Dionysus' servants, and so King Midas let him stay in his palace till the Satyr felt well rested.
